About

Introduction

Currently, I'm a volunteer for the county, helping out in the IT Division. I've been working with the web development team for almost a year now, updating content and making pages for their intranet and public site.

Volunteering set me on the path towards front end development. It sparked a passion for all things web related. Web technology is rapidly advancing so an exciting time to jump into the fray. There's a lot to learn, but I love that moment when you stumble on something new or cool that makes you go "You can do that?".

I'm also interest in the design aspect of user interfaces. I hold strongly to the idea that good design doesn't feel designed, but is intuitive to use for the user, allowing them to focus on the content.

Keeping a positive mindset is important to me. It's energizing and helps keep you motivated, even when the going gets tough.

More About Me

What’s your background?

I started programming in college and got a bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I took a break after graduating to figure out what I wanted from life. As luck would have it, I ended up volunteering, which set me on a path to finding out the answer.

Where I'm From

I'm from the West Coast. Born, raised, and currently living in California. The best part is that the beach is just a short drive away. Nothing beats making s'mores and sitting around a bonfire with good friends.

There's also a lot of diversity here, from the people to the melting pot of cultures. There's no shortage of sources for inspiration and creativity.

What’s your dream job?

I'm still in the process of figuring this out. Whatever it is, it involves working with a creative group of individuals developing something people will love or find useful, or hopefully both.
